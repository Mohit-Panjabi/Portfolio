The Institute Innovation Council (IIC) is an initiative of the Ministry of Human Resource Development (MHRD), Government of India, aimed at promoting innovation and entrepreneurship among higher education institutions across the country. The IIC provides a platform for colleges and universities to foster innovation and entrepreneurial culture, promote idea generation, and create a network of innovators, entrepreneurs, and mentors. Through various programs and initiatives, the IIC supports and encourages students and faculty to take up innovative projects, startup ventures, and develop prototypes that solve real-world problems.
